# Verb list

## Present tense
### Ith, "eat"
ithim - I eat
itheann - you/he/she/it/they eat
ithimid - we eat

### Ól, "drink"
ólaim - I drink
ólann - you/he/she/it/they drink
ólaimid - we drink

### Léamh, "read"
léim - I read
léann - you/he/she/it/they read
léimid - we read

### ???, "speak"
labhraim - I speak
labhraionn - you/he/she/it/they speaks
labhraimid - we speak

is - is, are
